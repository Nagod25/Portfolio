About
Joseph is a software developer intern who has built projects with HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Vanilla), Express.JS, Node.JS, and MongoDB. He is passionate about using tech for the improvement of every sector of human endeavor.

He has spent the last few years building websites for companies and organizations, including Youth Advocacy Program Africa, Missions 1, Asym Technologies, etc. His comprehensive knowledge of graphic design and various content management systems makes it easy for him to build completely customized websites with advanced features.

Joseph has worked on short-term initiatives with well-known NGOs such as the World Health Organization and the United States Agency for International Development. While serving as a youth corper in Obubra, Cross River State, he co-founded the Foundation for Social Development (FSD), an all-around human capacity development NGO.

Joseph Olusegun graduated from the University of Ilorin in Kwara State, Nigeria with a degree in Biochemistry.
